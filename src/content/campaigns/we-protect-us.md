Free Bystander Intervention Training we protect us Join Level Up’s free online bystander intervention training and learn how to safely interrupt harassment in public, at work, in your family or in your community. We provide practical, feminist-led training sessions that equip you with the skills to intervene in cases of sexist, racist, LGBTQ+ or Islamophobic harassment and gender-based violence. What the training is The fight for gender justice takes real action on a daily basis, but sometimes it’s hard to know what that looks like. That’s why Level Up runs free online bystander intervention sessions – to give everyone the skills they need to intervene when they see harassment happening. In the past year, we’ve trained 413 people, and we’re running more sessions in 2023! Why we run it Violence in our society – from sexist or racist harassment and domestic abuse to LGBTQ+ or Islamophobic hate crime – will not be prevented through more policing.
